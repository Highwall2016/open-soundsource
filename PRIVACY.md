# Privacy Policy

OpenSoundSource does not collect, store, sell, or transmit personal data.

OpenSoundSource uses macOS audio permissions only to capture audio from selected local applications and route that audio to selected local output devices. Audio is processed locally on your Mac. Audio is not recorded, saved, uploaded, or shared.

OpenSoundSource does not use analytics, tracking, advertising identifiers, or third-party telemetry.

If you have privacy questions, open an issue at:

https://github.com/Highwall2016/open-soundsource/issues
